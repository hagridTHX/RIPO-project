• Auto-expiration after 0.4 seconds (set in process_landmarks line 31) achieves:
  - Prevents false swipe detections from stale historical data
  - Allows complete swipe motion to be captured in wrist_history
  - Automatically handles motion blur at 60 FPS without blocking gestures

NOTE ON 60 FPS CONSIDERATIONS:
At 60 FPS (16.7ms per frame), camera and processing can miss individual frames due to:
- Motion blur (fast hand movement)
- Processing delays
- System resource contention

Traditional frame-by-frame clearing would lose swipe motion data. 
Time-based auto-expiration preserves motion information while preventing stale data.
